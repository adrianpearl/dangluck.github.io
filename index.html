About Dan

Dan received his BS in Athletic Training from the College of Health and Human Services at the University of New Hampshire in May of 2016. While at UNH, Dan played lacrosse and was named to the MCLA all-conference team and received the Scholar Athlete award his junior and senior years. During the summer of Dan's junior year he went to Israel where he played in the Israel's Premier Lacrosse League (IPLL). Post college, Dan also played for Team Israel during the Israel Lacrosse Boston Showcase, playing against Harvard, Boston University and Brown Division I Men's lacrosse teams in the fall of 2019.

Dan is a Certified Athletic Trainer licensed by the state of Massachusetts, CPR/AED certified, and is a Certified Strength and Conditioning Specialist by the National Strength and Conditioning Association.

In spring of 2019 Dan started his first college strength coach internship with Harvard's men's hockey, lacrosse, football and basketball teams. Following Harvard Dan became a head strength coach at Edge Performance Systems, during his time there he also had his first internship with Boston University Men's hockey, lacrosse and basketball teams. Dan was fortunate enough to work with Julian Edelman, his first New England Patriots player, during Julian's superbowl MVP season in 2019.

Dan left EPS in 2021 to pursue his master's degree at Boston University and work closely with Ken Whitter, former Bruins assistant strength coach and current head strength coach at BU men's hockey. During his full time internship with the men's hockey team he started working on the side with a new elite performance gym, Bando Performance. Dan later joined full time in the summer at the Worcester location training former Olympic hockey player and current railers captain Bobby Butler.

With the opening of Bando Performance's third location in Dedham, Dan became the head coach under Director/Owner Charlie Bando.

Dan has worked with many Olympians, Professional, College, and Prep/High School athletes over the last 7 years as a strength and conditioning coach and athletic trainer. In his spare time he likes to play hockey and lacrosse as well as enjoy a nice meal with friends and family.
