The Dan Gluck Training Experience

Personal Training/Nutrition Training

Get your personalized training and nutrition training program.

Together we work on a specific lifestyle plan to achieve your goals. Gaining strength, losing weight, or maintaining a specific body composition - our goal is whatever is best for you. This service incorporates exercise assessment, goal setting, fitness programming, nutritional education, safety, motivation, and accountability. We create fun and exciting fitness programs incorporating a variety of different exercises and fitness techniques. Workout the way you want and eat the foods you love.

Sport Specific Training

We create training plans specifically for your sport. We use periodization, sport-specific techniques, and recovery days to make sure you perform at your best when competition comes.

We make use of advanced testing techniques to effectively target areas most in need of improvement. We create individualized plans for any season of any sport. We want you to build skill and confidence to reach peak performance. We challenge the energy, muscular, and nervous systems to make you a more complete athlete.

Athletic Training/Active Recovery

Are you injury prone and need a preventative injury program? Do you have a chronic injury and need to know how to treat it?

We are fully certified and licensed in Athletic Training, meaning we have the ability to evaluate, diagnose, and provide treatment and rehabilitation for pain and injuries. We use a mix of preventative injury training, modalities and proper corrective exercises to help anyone out, not just the athletes. Our return to play guidelines properly prepare anyone that needs to return to their activity. This can be anything from getting ready for a weekend softball game after dealing with a past shoulder injury to just getting back to going on long hikes post concussion.

More About Dan

Dan graduated from South Burlington High School and received his BS in Athletic Training from the College of Health and Human Services at the University of New Hampshire. While at South Burlington High School, Dan played varsity hockey and lacrosse getting many awards such as offensive player of the year in hockey and was top LSM in Vermont making the Vermont coaches association first team all state. While at UNH, Dan was named to the MCLA all-conference team and received his scholar athlete award his junior and senior years. During the summer of Dan’s junior year in college, he went over to play in the inaugural year of Israel's Premier Lacrosse League. Post college, Dan also played for Team Israel during the Israel Lacrosse Boston Showcase, playing against Harvard, Boston University and Brown Division I Men’s lacrosse teams.

Dan is a Certified Athletic Trainer licensed by the state of Massachusetts, CPR/AED certified, and has his CSCS (certified strength and conditioning specialist) by the National Strength and Conditioning Association. Dan has interned with Harvard and Boston University strength and conditioning programs. Dan is currently working full time at Edge Performance Systems, New England's top strength and conditioning center. Dan has worked with numerous professional athletes including Julian Edelman during his Super Bowl MVP season.

In his free time, Dan enjoys playing in his adult hockey league, eating donuts, and having a good meal with friends and family.
